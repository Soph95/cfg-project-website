    • Brussels is one of the most beautiful cities, full of tourist attractions. In this guide you will see some of the best attractions, food places and events you can visit all in a day!

    Morning: St Michael and St Gudula Cathedral

    St Michael and St Gudula Cathedral

    How to arrive

    Be sure to take in the breathtaking views when you arrive!

    We suggest that you head for breakfast at 9am then to the Cathedral for 10am to make the most out of your visit.

    Address: Place Sainte-Gudule, 1000 Bruxelles, Belgium

    The Cathedral is located in the city of Brussels, to get directions and information on how to get there click on the map below!

    Recommendations

    What better way to visit a City than learning and seeing some of its history!

    There are many things to see inside and outside the Cathedral including, stain glass windows.

    If you fancy spending a more time at the Cathedral, why not stay for the mass service.

    Food

    Whilst visiting St Michael and St Gudula Cathedral, there are plenty of great food places you can enjoy around the area, ranging from traditional Belgium cafes to cooked breakfast.

    Evening: Grand Palace and Walk around city

    Grand Palace_ Brussels

    How to arrive

    Be sure to take lots of pictures as you see the grand palace be lit up!

    We suggest that you have a walk around town from 6pm, followed by Dinner and be at the Grand Palace by 9:30pm

    Address: Grand Place Brussels Main Square, Brussels 1000 Belgium

    Click on the image of the map below to see directions!

    Recommendations

    There is a lot to see around the city of Brussels and plenty of photo oportunities!

    When walking around the city be sure to keep your eye out for the Manneken Pis!

    If you have time be sure to visit the city museum to learn a bit about the history of Brussels!

    Address: Grand Place 2, 1000 Bruxelles, Belgium
